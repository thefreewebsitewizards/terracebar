Our Philosophy

Located in the vibrant heart of Tbilisi, Terrace Bar offers more than just a view. It offers a perspective. We believe that great food tastes better in the open air, surrounded by the pulsating energy of the city.

Our culinary team reimagines Georgian classics with a contemporary twist. We honor the deep-rooted traditions of our region while embracing modern techniques that highlight the natural flavors of our locally sourced ingredients.

The Crowd Favorite

Signature Adjarian Khachapuri

Our take on the boat-shaped classic. We use a blend of three local cheeses, organic egg yolk, and a generous pat of mountain butter. Baked in our stone oven until the crust is golden and crisp, while the center remains molten and rich.
