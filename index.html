About Terrace Bar
Our Story

Dining Under the Stars

Authentic Georgian Flavors in a Modern Oasis

Welcome to Terrace Bar, where tradition meets the electric energy of the night. Our vibrant terrace oasis invites you to escape the city noise and immerse yourself in the warmth of true Georgian hospitality.

We believe in the power of farm-fresh ingredients. From our signature cheesy Khachapuri pulled straight from the oven to our locally sourced herbs and meats, every dish tells a story of authenticity and passion.

Whether you're here for a romantic evening or a lively gathering with friends, the cozy atmosphere and open sky create the perfect backdrop for memories that last.
